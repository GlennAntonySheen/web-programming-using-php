AIR JORDAN

Air jordan is our state of the art sneakers inspired by Hall of Fame Basketball player and six-time NBA Finals MVP Michael Jordan.The original Air Jordan sneakers were produced exclusively for Michael Jordan in late 1984, and released to the public on April 1, 1985. So far 35 different versions of air jordans have been released.The shoes were designed for Nike by Peter Moore, Tinker Hatfield, and Bruce Kilgore.
